# Wizard Pond Prototype Art Style Guide

## Direction

Wizard Pond should look like a stylized low-poly / pixel-inspired 3D fantasy roguelite.

Use simple readable shapes first:

- Low-poly 3D silhouettes
- Pixel-inspired colors and material blocks
- Chunky fantasy props
- Bright magical village mood
- Cozy pond environment
- Funky wizard clothing
- Playful pond mobs and boss fish
- Simple materials with bold colors

## Mood Words

- Cozy fantasy
- Arcade roguelite
- Colorful wizard village
- Magical pond
- Simple polygon models
- Pixelated texture feel
- Readable from a distance
- Fun and goofy, not realistic

## Placeholder Geometry

- Player and NPCs: capsules
- Mobs: capsules, spheres, squashed cubes
- Houses: cubes with prism-like roofs
- Water: flat planes with blue/cyan material
- Ground: large planes with green material
- Trees/posts: cylinders
- Mushrooms/crystals/lanterns/signs: spheres, cones, cubes, cylinders
- Docks: long thin cubes

## Village Areas

- Main Pond: central pond, lily pads, frogs, turtles, dock, fishing spot, run portal.
- Wizard Village: wizard hut, shop stall, upgrade table, wardrobe tent, signs, lanterns.
- Wild Edge: mob area, camp, mushrooms, crystals, broken dock, path back to village.
- Boss Gate: magical arch, glowing crystals, inactive boss entrance marker.

## NPC Identity

- Froggin the Bait Merchant: green shopkeeper near the dock.
- Shellbert the Turtle Sage: slow wise trainer near turtle rocks.
- Mira Moonhook: rod upgrade NPC near the shop stall.
- Glimmer Gob: cosmetic vendor near the wardrobe tent.
- Old Man Ripple: lore NPC near the campfire.

## Pixel-Inspired Presentation

- Use flat materials with no texture or tiny pixel-style textures.
- Set texture Filter Mode to Point when using pixel art textures.
- Use simple directional light and soft ambient light.
- Use chunky props and strong color contrast instead of realistic detail.
- Optional later: render the camera to a low-resolution Render Texture and upscale it with Point filtering.

## Suggested Colors

- Village grass: bright moss green
- Water: cyan-blue with slight transparency
- Magic accents: magenta, violet, neon cyan, gold
- Wizard clothes: bold robe colors with contrasting hat bands
- Mobs: green, purple, muddy blue
- Boss fish markers: gold, orange, neon, rainbow

## Prototype Rule

Do not wait for final art. Build the scene with primitives, clear colors, and readable silhouettes first. Replace pieces later only after the game loop feels fun.
